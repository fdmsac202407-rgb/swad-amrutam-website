Our Story

એક સારી ચા માટે સારી પત્તી, સારી પસંદગી અને સાચી કાળજી જરૂરી છે.

Swad Amrutam was created for chai lovers who want a strong yet balanced cup. Our focus is not only on selling tea patti, but on building a product that becomes part of daily family moments.

From selecting tea leaves to packing, every step is handled with attention so customers receive consistent taste, aroma and freshness.

To offer a dependable premium tea product that gives daily chai lovers a strong taste, fresh aroma and confidence in every pack.
